Geography

Where is Nueva Vizcaya?

Nueva Vizcaya is a landlocked province situated in the southern part of the Cagayan Valley region (Region II) in Luzon, Philippines. It is bounded by Quirino and Aurora to the east, Pangasinan and Benguet to the west, Ifugao and Mountain Province to the north, and Nueva Ecija to the south.

The province serves as the gateway between the Cagayan Valley and Central Luzon. Its capital town is Bayombong.

How to Get There

By Bus

Regular bus lines from Manila offer trips to Bayombong. Travel time is 5–7 hours.

By Private Vehicle

Take NLEX north then Maharlika Highway through Nueva Ecija to Bayombong.

By Air

Fly to Cauayan Airport (Isabela), then travel 2 hours by land to Bayombong.
