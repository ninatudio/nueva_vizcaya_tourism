Conquer the Peaks of Nueva Vizcaya

Mountain Destinations

Nueva Vizcaya's rugged terrain is home to some of the most spectacular trekking destinations in Northern Luzon.

⛰️ Trekking Tips

Preparation

Always register with local tourism offices before hiking. Hiring a local guide is mandatory for most peaks.

What to Bring

Bring enough water, food, first aid kit, rain gear, and warm clothing. Temperatures drop significantly at higher elevations.

Leave No Trace

Pack out all your trash. Respect the environment and local communities. Do not disturb wildlife or pick plants.
