Reasons to Visit Nueva Vizcaya

Whether you're seeking adventure, culture, or tranquility, Nueva Vizcaya delivers it all.

⛰️
World-Class Mountains

Home to parts of the Cordillera ranges, the province offers some of the best mountain trekking in the Philippines, including legendary peaks like Mount Ugo and Kayapa Quad Peak.

🕳️
Spectacular Cave Systems

The Capisaan Cave System is one of the most extensive in the Philippines, offering an unforgettable spelunking adventure through dramatic underground chambers.

💧
Pristine Waterfalls

From Imugan to Mapalyao, the province's waterfalls are accessible, beautiful, and often uncrowded — perfect for a peaceful escape into nature.

🍊
Citrus Capital of the Philippines

Sample the freshest citrus fruits directly from highland farms. Nueva Vizcaya is celebrated for its sweet oranges, ponkans, and other citrus varieties that are exported nationwide.

🎭
Rich Indigenous Culture

Encounter living indigenous cultures of the Bugkalot, Gaddang, Isinai, and Kalanguya — communities that maintain their traditional music, weaving, and rituals to this day.

🎉
The Ammungan Festival

Every April, the province comes alive with one of the grandest cultural festivals in Region II, featuring street dances, indigenous performances, and vibrant trade fairs.

😊
Warm & Welcoming People

Nueva Vizcayans are known for their warmth and genuine hospitality. Visitors consistently describe the people as among the friendliest they've encountered in their travels.

🌡️
Cool Highland Climate

Escape the lowland heat in the cool mountain towns of Santa Fe, Kayapa, and Ambaguio. The refreshing highland air makes outdoor activities especially enjoyable.

💰
Budget-Friendly Destination

Compared to more crowded tourist spots, Nueva Vizcaya offers incredible natural and cultural experiences at very reasonable prices — great value for every type of traveler.
